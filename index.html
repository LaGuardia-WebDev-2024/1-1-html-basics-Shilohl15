Red Flower

A poem (soon to be song) that highlights a beautiful red flower.
That flower doesn't realize how amazingly red its petals are so in turn it criticizes itself.

Flower why do you criticize your bright red petals
You outshine all the little daffodils that tell you you're too bright
Youre pretty stature and stance knocks all the boring little daffodils out of the park
But the most important thing I have to say is dont let that crunchy leaf tell you you're too bright
